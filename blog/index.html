the intention

Food has become too addictive. Aggressive amounts of sodium and refined sugars have dulled our ability to taste the ingredients themselves. Often, what we crave is not the food, but the seasoning. This is a guide to resetting the palate and rediscovering true flavor—focusing on the intentional use of seasonings rather than a reliance on them.
