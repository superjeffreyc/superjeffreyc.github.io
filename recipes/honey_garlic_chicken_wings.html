Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Put 2 slits on each side of the chicken wings
  2. Put chicken wings in a bowl
  3. Add sauce, seasoning, and chopped garlic to the bowl
  4. Marinate for 2 hours

Steps

  1. Add chicken wings and sauce into wok, no oil needed
  2. Mix well and cook until done
  3. Taste and season as needed
