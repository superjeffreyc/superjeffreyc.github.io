Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken (any meat can substitute)
  • Kimchi
  • Garlic Powder
  • Red Pepper Paste
  • Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooked Rice
  • Onions (optional)
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Canola oil

Instructions

  1. Cook rice early or use leftover rice
  2. Cut garlic, onions, kimchi, and meat to small bite size pieces
  3. Put canola oil into pan
  4. Put garlic into pan
  5. Let garlic turn golden brown
  6. Once garlic is happy, add kimchi
  7. Once kimchi loses its color, add cooked meat
  8. Put in red pepper paste, soy sauce, and sesasme oil into the pan or wok and mix
  9. Cook eggs, scoop into seperate bowl, and set aside
  10. Add rice to the pan or wok and mix
  11. Add egs last and mix
  12. Mix everything evenly
  13. Serve and enjoy
