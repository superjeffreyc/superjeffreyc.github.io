Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder)
  3. Mix wet ingredients in a different bowl (canola oil, egg, milk, vanilla extract)
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients
  5. Add blueberries
  6. Mix well, but do not overmix
  7. Place batter into 12 muffin cups
  8. Bake for 20 minutes
