Beef Chow Fun

Ingredients

  • Chow Fun
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Seasoned Beef (any meat can be substituted, raw or cooked)
  • Bean Sprout
  • Enoki Mushroom (optional)
  • Canola Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up the Chow Fun in a microwave to soften it
  2. Add canola oil to wok, wait for oil to heat up, and stir fry beef (if not already cooked)
  3. Once meat is done, add bean sprouts and any other vegetables
  4. Add softened Chow Fun
  5. Add oyster sauce to taste
  6. Mix well and taste and season as needed
