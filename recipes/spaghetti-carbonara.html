Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in a pot
  2. Cook bacon in a pan until crisp
  3. Move bacon to a separate bowl and remove excess oil from pan
  4. Add heavy cream, cheese, and a dash of salt.
  5. Stir until all the cheese is combined into a creamy sauce
  6. Add pasta and bacon and mix well
  7. Add a generous amount of black pepper on top before serving
