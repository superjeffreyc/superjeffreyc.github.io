Blueberry Muffin

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all purpose flour (important: do not pack flour when measuring or muffins will not be fluffy)
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup blueberries (frozen or fresh)

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  • Mix dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder)
  • Mix wet ingredients in a different bowl (canola oil, egg, milk, vanilla extract)
  • Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients
  • Add blueberries
  • Mix well, but do not overmix
  • Place batter into 12 muffin cups
  • Bake for 20 minutes
