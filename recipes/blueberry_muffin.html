Blueberry Muffin

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all purpose flour (important: do not pack flour when measuring or muffins will not be fluffy)
  • 1/3 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup blueberries (frozen or fresh)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder)
  3. Mix wet ingredients in a different bowl (canola oil, egg, milk, vanilla extract)
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients
  5. Add blueberries
  6. Mix well, but do not overmix
  7. Place batter into 12 muffin cups
  8. Bake for 20 minutes
